Game Rules (Summary)

1) Roles
- You are the red crosshair (human).
- The agent is the orange crosshair.
- Work together to hit the falling target.

2) Controls
- Move: left stick.
- Shoot: A button (only when the target is inside the inner ring).

3) Scoring
- Score +1 when the target is hit by either side.
- Conflicts +1 when crosshairs overlap and cause interference.
- Errors +1 when the target falls past the bottom line.
